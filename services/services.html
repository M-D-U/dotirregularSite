Team Building Packages

Not everyone is at ease in more traditional networking settings, where it can be difficult to engage in meaningful discussions. Conversations can feel brief, repetitive, and superficial. And for the shy and introverted, unstructured mingling can become stressful fast.

But because team building activities focus on an individual's ability to form alliances, they're great for building trust and teamwork and offer an exciting way to make friends and communicate better at work.

At Dot.Irregular Media and Sound Hire, we offer a unique team building experience by combining team building activities/games with music to help quickly reduce tension and encourage engagement with colleagues.

We offer team building packages for different types of groups, from indoor-based to outdoor-based.

INDOOR PACKAGE

Rely on our indoor package to get your team working together to solve problems.

  • Trivias
  • Brain teaser/Geography Puzzles
  • Jenga/Block Tower
  • Classic mini Table Top Games (pool table, air hockey & table soccer)
  • Buzz Wire

OUTDOOR PACKAGE

You can trust our outdoor package to instigate teamwork and a competitive edge.

  • Table tennis/Ping Pong
  • Mini soccer
  • Tug of war
  • Blindfold racing and other fun games
